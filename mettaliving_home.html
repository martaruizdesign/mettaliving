Surround yourself with beauty and you will feel beautiful. Create a space of comfort and you will feel nurtured. Live amongst order and you will feel balanced. Metta Living offers a variety of ways to enhance your home, making it one that emanates the architecture of your soul. Offering workshops and private consultations.

Art, Yoga, health and travel are passions that have infused Carolyn's life since her teens. Her education in Fine Art led her to a successful career in home design and the decorative arts. Through years of intensive studies – she has earned multiple certifications in Yoga, Bodywork and Ayurveda - Carolyn's spiritual practices have given her an inner stability while motivating her to share her knowledge and creative energy with others.

Exploring 25 countries in 30 years, Carolyn has shifted her world view to one that embodies trust and interconnection. Her various destinations to places of rich cultural heritage have inspired her to lead others, sharing her experiences while encouraging individuals in their own journey.

Metta Living invites you to investigate ways to enhance and enjoy your life with a deeper level of connection, to yourself and the world.

"Life gives us amazing opportunities to explore and integrate vast amounts of information and sensuous experiences. With mindfulness, or intentional living, we can create from these opportunities sanctuaries in space and time not only for ourselves, but for everyone around us".

Are you longing for a place in your home conducive to your practice- whether it is yoga, meditation, journaling or playing music? This workshop will teach you how to bring beauty and balance into your home. We will focus on making changes to one specific area that will support your creative or spiritual practice and bring positive energy to the overall space.

You will learn how to chose the right location and what elements to implement for your individual needs. We will learn how to cultivate a healthy interior through identification of sattvic, rajasic and tamasic environments. Color, aromas and other methods of feeding the sense object will also be presented. With inspiration from this workshop, you will feel more connected to your home and your true self.

“Creating a sacred space” is for people who are looking for ways to incorporate their spiritual life into their home surroundings. Whether you want to dedicate a small corner, or a whole room, you will learn the steps needed to start the transformation.

Art of the Altar

Spring Workshop:TBD
or Book a consultation

What defines an altar? Where would you place one? What do you need in order to create one?

Altars are personal. They are created with specific intentions, different for each individual. They can connect us to spirit, encourage contemplation; they may be used for healing, prayer or centering.

This workshop explores the different ways altars can live in your home or office. We will begin the process of how to create one that is specific to you and where you may look for the objects you will place on it. Join us for a fun exploration of our hearts and souls and how they can help us in the manifestation of the altar.
