From the jet-setting island of Capri to the stunning Amalfi towns, from ancient Pompeii to even more ancient Paestum, this is Italy's coast with the most. Stretching along the southern side of the Sorrentine Peninsula, the Amalfi Coast is one of Europe’s most breathtaking. Cliffs terraced with scented lemon groves reach down to sparkling seas; white-washed villas cling to the vertical terrain. Beautiful sandy coves tease from far below and out of reach. Today the Amalfi Coast is one of Italy’s top tourist destinations, a favorite of well-to-do jet-setters and couples seeking romance.

Read Rick Steve's Europe-Italy: Naples, Sorrento, and the Amalfi Coast and Introducing Amalfi Coast for more information.
